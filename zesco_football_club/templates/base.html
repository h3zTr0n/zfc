About FC

The Club is registered under office of Registrar of Companies and Business names and is therefore referred to as Zesco United Football Club Limited. The club is professionally run with all the Players, Technical Bench and Secretariat Staff being on full time employment basis. ZUFC is affiliated to the Football Association of Zambia (FAZ), Confederation of African Football (CAF) and the Federation of International Football Association (FIFA). The club has pledged full compliance to the Club Licensing regulations and is committed to taking part in all forums organized by FAZ, CAF and FIFA for the implementation of Club Licensing.
